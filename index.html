R4Consultancy on TalentC

R4Consultancy supports and endorses TalentC Mentoring Program, the only ‘world wide’ Accredited Training Organization (ATO) to deliver the International Certification for Mentoring.

TalentC has been pre-approved as a recertification credit provider by the Human Resources Certification Institute (HRCI) to deliver ICM (International Certification for Mentoring) program to the 250,000 members of the Society of Human Resources Management in the United States of America and 140 member countries around the world. “Making you successful is what matters”-TalentC.

Level 1 and Level 2 Mentor Training courses are eligible for recertification points with their relevant designations by the following organizations:

  • Human Resources Institute of Alberta (CHRP)
  • Saskatchewan Association of Human Resource Professionals (CHRP)
  • International Personnel Management Association - Canada (IPMA-CP)
  • British Columbia Human Resources Management Association
  • Human Resource Management Association of Manitoba (HRMAM)
